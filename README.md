## What I'm Building

### [Fluidify Regen](https://github.com/FluidifyAI/Regen) — Open-Source, Agent-Native Incident Management

After nearly a decade of living inside incident timelines, I got tired of watching teams throw expensive SaaS tooling at a problem that's fundamentally about workflow and context.

**Fluidify Regen** is a self-hosted incident management platform built for the age of AI agents.

Most incident tools were designed before AI agents were part of the stack. They bolt on integrations as an afterthought. Fluidify is different — it's **agent-native from day one**, using **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** as the integration standard. Your observability tools, on-call workflows, and AI agents speak the same language out of the box.

**Why it matters:**
- No vendor lock-in, no per-seat pricing for features you've already built workarounds for
- Full control over your incident data and automation logic
- Designed to work *with* your agents, not around them
- A credible open-source alternative to incident.io for teams that want to own their stack

> Built by SREs who've been on the wrong end of a 2am alert one too many times.
